About

Mococo Abyssguard is the twin sister of Fuwawa Abyssguard and makes up half of the VTuber duo "FUWAMOCO". The pair debuted for the VTuber agency "Hololive" in July, 2023. Mococo uses the knowledge and experience gained from her position in the Demon World as the Demon World Gate protector to protect her fans' smiles. Her name is a play on the Japanese word "moko moko", meaning fluffy. Be sure not to mistake her for her sister!
